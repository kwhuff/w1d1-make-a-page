Arsenal football club were founded in 1886 by a group of workers employed by the Dial Square workshop at the Royal Arenal, an armaments factory if Woolwich, Kent (formally part of the new county of London from 1889). They were led by a Scotsman, David Danskin, who purchased the club's first football, and Jack Humble. Presently, they are sponsored by Emirates Airline.
